About this extension

Draft Genius plugs a writing skill directly into your agents. Point it at a goal — a launch post, a docs page, a weekly digest — and it drafts in your voice, cites its sources, and hands you a run you can inspect, edit, and replay. It never publishes without your say-so.

Every draft carries full provenance, so you always know which brief, which style guide, and which model produced each paragraph.

Features

Voice-matched drafts

Learns your style guide and writes to it — every time.

Inline citations

Sources attached to every claim, ready to verify.

Replayable runs

Inspect every step and re-run with one edit.

Human-in-the-loop

Nothing ships until you approve — the veto is always yours.
